MATTHIEU DE BOISSÉSON
Arbitrator
Arbitration Chambers, London, Singapore, New York (Member)
Littleton Chambers, London (Door Tenant)
Admitted in Paris

mdb@boissesonarbitration.com    

Since 1985, Matthieu de Boisséson has been actively involved as a counsel, arbitrator or legal expert in more than 350 international arbitration cases in all parts of the world. He has acted in the fields of mergers and acquisitions, construction, oil and gas, joint-ventures, investments disputes, defence contracts, telecom and high technologies, distribution, in ICC, SCC, LCIA, PCA, ICSID and other arbitral institutions cases, as well as ad hoc cases.

He has been a partner of Darrois Villey Maillot Brochier in Paris from 1995 to 2013 and a partner of Linklaters from 2013 to 2016, as Co-Head of International Arbitration Worldwide. Mr. de Boisséson has given lectures or conferences on international arbitration in various Universities and institutions around the world. Since 2016, Mr.de Boisséson has been a full-time international arbitrator. He is a member of Arbitration Chambers (London, Singapore, New York) and is a Door Tenant of Littleton Chambers (London).

He is a member of numerous arbitration associations. He is the author of "Le droit français de l’arbitrage interne et international" (3 rd edition to be published at LGDJ in 2022) and numerous articles on international arbitrations. He speaks fluent French, English, Portuguese and has a good understanding of Spanish.
